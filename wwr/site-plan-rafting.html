Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to build revenue for a parent company, and to share information for those interested in white water rafting.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Adults that are seeking a thrill. Likely those that are physically fit and enjoy the outdoors. What do they want? They want the opportunity to raft down rapids and have an enjoyable time. How will they access my website? Laptop or mobile device.
